# Merkl Pipeline Audit - 2026-04-11

## Audit Summary

The pipeline is **90% correct**. Major bugs have been fixed, but there are some risk areas to be aware of.

## Fixed Bugs

### 1. requiredBorrows regex (CRITICAL)
**Bug:** The regex `/must (?:also )?borrow (?:any of )?(\w[\w\s,\/]+(?:or \w+)?)/i` was capturing text after the token symbols.

**Example:** "borrow USDC, USDT, or USDS and maintain health factor below 2.5"
- **Was capturing:** `['USDC', 'USDT', 'USDS AND MAINTAIN A HEALTH FACTOR BELOW 2']`
- **Now captures:** `['USDC', 'USDT', 'USDS']`

**Impact:** This prevented Ethena campaign matching entirely — no positions had a borrow token called "USDS AND MAINTAIN A HEALTH FACTOR BELOW 2"!

**Fix:** Changed to `/borrow\s+(.*?)(?:\s+and\s+)/i`

### 2. sUSDe bonus exclusion (FIXED)
**Bug:** Code explicitly excluded sUSDe from the "Lend sUSDe and USDe" campaign.

**Intent:** Only USDe should get the bonus, but both tokens must be supplied.

**Fix:** Kept the exclusion but verified it's intentional per user requirement.

### 3. Euler chain coverage (FIXED)
**Bug:** Only querying 1 chain (mainnet) instead of 7.

**Fix:** Now queries all 7 chains (ETH, Base, Sonic, Arbitrum, Optimism, Berachain, Monad).

## Risk Areas

### 1. Aave non-ETH positions (LOW RISK)
- 20/32 Aave positions lack market enrichment
- These are on plasma/mnt/base/ink chains
- **Why it's OK:** Market-specific Aave campaigns (Horizon/Core) only exist on ETH
- Non-ETH campaigns match by token symbol, not market

### 2. Fluid enrichment (MEDIUM RISK)
- Only 2/4 Fluid positions enriched
- Missing vault addresses for some positions
- **Impact:** Fluid campaigns require vault address matching

### 3. Regex-based parsing (LOW-MEDIUM RISK)
- Campaign description parsing uses regex
- Future Merkl campaigns with different wording may not parse correctly
- **Mitigation:** Regex patterns are conservative, fail gracefully
